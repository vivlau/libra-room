ABOUT

Welcome to the Libra Room, the only spot in Vancouver to catch live music seven-nights a week. Now in its tenth year of transformation, the Libra Room – located in the heart of Commercial Drive – has carved out its own unique niche in the community.

Like the neighbourhood it’s situated in, the space is colourfully eclectic. Wooden tables, chairs, centre-bar and performance stage add a rustic charm to the perennial haunt. Works from local artists decorate the walls. Each month a new artist is featured with the pieces available for purchase.

The Libra Room is home to young burgeoning musicians and Vancouver jazz giants such as John Korsrud, Wes Mackey and Bruno Hubert. Two bands perform nightly, taking the stage at 6:30 pm and 9 pm respectively. Supporting the local music scene, the Libra Room has played host to major musical events, such as the New Music West festival, The Juno Festival and the Vancouver Jazz Festival.
